  • I am an observational seismologist, primarily interested in earthquake and non-earthquake seismic source processes. By using seismic records from global and local seismic networks, we are developing earthquake-source imaging techniques and array processing methods to resolve diverse rupture behaviours of the earthquake and detect/locate/analyse peculiar non-earthquake, sub-surficial seismic sources, aiming at better understanding of kinematics and physics of such energetic Earth’s dynamics.
